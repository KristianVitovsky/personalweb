    What will we try in the upocoming sessions?

    To move forward I aim to try some of the following in the tutorial:

  • Open the session with lecture's "best hits", that will be an opportunity for anyone to recap one of the core ideas of the preceding week's lecture that caught their attention.
  • We will spend a significant amount of time going back over the concepts of the lecture. Especially focusing on the questions that we are trying to answer, assumptions anf the meaning of the results.
  • Along the way, we will answer questions suggested by the students.
  • We will be thorough in motivating the problems from the exercise sheets and others.
  • The exercise sheet problems themselves will not be omitted, yet we will only return to the key steps.
