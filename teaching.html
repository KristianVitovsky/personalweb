Primary school: PJZŠ Horáčkova

  • Until the end of the 2022/2023 school year, I taught physics and informatics at PJZŠ Horáčkova school in Prague. During the COVID-19 pandemic, the school moved to an online format. Throughout the first school term, I created video lessons and accompanying materials, which I posted for my students on the following website: vitovskyuci.webnode.cz (in Czech). Later on, the school transitioned to using MS Teams for the subsequent pandemic terms.
